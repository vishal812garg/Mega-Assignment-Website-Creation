Historical Places in India....

Ajanta and Ellora Caves in Maharashtra.Both of them are UNESCO World Heritage Site..
Mysore Palace, Mysore.It was build by the British architect Lord Henry Irwin in 1912............Mysore Palace during the Dasara festival is a spectacular sight.During this occasion,the entire palace is illuminated with over 1,00,000 lights.
Hampi,karnataka.The ruins of Hampi,a UNESCO World Heritage Site...Hampi was bulit by the great ruler DevaRayaII of Vijaynagara Empire..
Amer Fort,Rajasthan.It's bulit with both red and white sandstone.and stands as an example of ancient INdian architecture..The greatest attraction in the Amer Fort is the Sheesh Mahal(mirror palace).
Taj Mahal,AgraUNESCO World Heritage Site in India. took around 22 years to complete, and no wonder, the effort of every year is reflected in its construction.
Fatehpur Sikri, Agraa UNESCO World Heritage Site, Bulid during Akbar,this royal city is home to several buildings like Birbal's Palace,Tomb of Salim Chisti and Jama Masjid.
